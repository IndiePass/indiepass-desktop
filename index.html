About

Indigenous allows you to engage with the internet as you do on social media sites, but posts it all on your website. Use the built-in reader to read and respond to posts across the internet. Indigenous doesn\'t track or store any of your information. You choose a service you trust or host it yourself.

Development happens on GitHub

Keyboard shortcuts

  • Reader
    • p: previous post in feed or overlay
    • n: next post in feed or overlay, triggers 'More posts' at end
    • r or z: read the post in the overlay
    • c or esc: close overlay when opened
